Our Story

A Space Where
Coffee Meets Craft

May's Coffee was born from a simple conviction: that great coffee deserves a great environment. We designed every corner of our space — from the warm wood-slat counters to the handpicked pendant lights — to make you feel at home the moment you walk in.

Every bean we source comes with a story. We visit farms, build relationships, and roast in small batches so that what lands in your cup is nothing short of extraordinary. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just coffee done right.

Why May's Coffee

Crafted With
Intention

Single Origin Beans

Every bean is sourced from a single farm, preserving the unique character of its origin. We visit farms to build lasting relationships with growers worldwide.

Small Batch Roasted

Roasted in controlled small batches to lock in peak flavour. No warehousing, no stale beans — each roast is calibrated by our head roaster to bring out the best.

7 Brew Methods

From hand-drip to full immersion, our baristas are trained in seven brewing methods. Every technique unlocks a different dimension of flavour from the same bean.

Our Space

Architecture
Meets Aroma

The space was designed as a living experience — concrete textures, warm timber accents, the glow of our signature sun-circle installation, and a double-height atrium filled with natural light and the scent of freshly ground coffee.
